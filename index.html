How it works

Three steps. No account.

Install, track, get out of the way. Your list lives in your browser, not on a server.

Step 01

Track a product.

Browse to any Amazon product page and hit Track Product. That's the whole setup.

Step 02

We watch it.

SaleCheck checks the price once a day, quietly, in the background. Nothing runs on pages you visit.

Step 03

You get a nudge.

When the price drops, a small badge appears on the extension icon. Open it, see the deal, decide.

Deal intelligence

Know a real deal when you see one.

Every price is scored against a 90-day history. You see if today's number is a historic low, a good price, or a retailer pretending to discount something that's been this price for weeks.
