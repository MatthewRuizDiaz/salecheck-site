How It Works

Three steps. No account. No setup. Just savings.

Step 1

Track a Product

Browse to any product page and click "Track This Product" in the extension. That's it.

Step 2

We Monitor Daily

SaleCheck checks prices automatically every day. No action required on your part.

Step 3

Get Notified

When a price drops, a badge appears on the extension icon. Open it, see the deal, and buy at the right time.

Built for Real Shoppers

No Account Required

Install and start tracking immediately. No sign-up, no email, no password. Your data stays in your browser.

Automatic Daily Checks

Prices are refreshed every day in the background. You only hear from us when there's a deal worth knowing about.

Multi-Retailer Support

Track products across Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Macy's, and more — all in one place.

Visual Sale Indicators

Color-coded discount tiers — gold for 55%+ off, silver for 40%+, orange for 20%+. Spot the best deals instantly.

Drag, Sort, Rename

Organize your tracked products however you like. Rename titles, drag to reorder, sort by price or discount.

Privacy First

No personal data collected. No user accounts on our servers. Your tracked products live in your browser, not our database.
